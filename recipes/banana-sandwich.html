The Banana Sandwich

Description

This simple sandwich will transport you back to child. The sweet banana and the tangy mayonaise are the perfect combination to bring back memories of summer days and mom's kitchen

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 2 slices bread
  • Mayonaise
  • For extra credit: Peanut butter

Steps

  1. Slice bananas
  2. Add mayonaise to both sides of the bread
  3. Place banana on bread
  4. Enjoy!
  5. for extra credit, add peanut butter on one side of the bread instead mayo
