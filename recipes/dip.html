Vommit Dip

Description

This cheesy, somewhat spicy, deliciousness might look like someone had a little too much vodka last night and decided to empty the contents of their stomach in to your bowl... but have faith! is the most addictive dip you'll ever make!

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Heat cream cheese in 15 sec blast, until it starts to soften
  2. As cream cheese begins to soften, add in salsa
